總覽 Executive Summary

What This Reading Does

讀本 Reading Scope

Focus: Ru fajie pin 入法界品 (Gandavyuha), the final movement of the Huayan corpus. We read it as literary architecture, not only as doctrine.

Pinyin first use: Huayan 華嚴, Shancai tongzi 善財童子 (Sudhana), Wenshu 文殊 (Manjushri), Puxian 普賢 (Samantabhadra), Maitreya 彌勒.

關鍵 Main Argument

The chapter produces spiritual scale through accumulation: vast numbers, impossible simultaneity, and serial encounters with many teachers across social boundaries.

Pedagogical aim: students should see that literary form carries ethical vision.

主題 Your Class Themes
  • Big numbers and cosmic counting
  • Infinity vocabulary: “infinite,” “countless,” “inconceivable”
  • Many teachers: laymen, laywomen, and marginalized figures
文本 Text Analysis

Close Reading Cards (With Page-Cited Quotes)

數量 Big Numbers
“...the plurality of spiritual paths that lead to the awakening of Buddhas, and the infinite opportunity...” — Gomez, p. 162 (course packet translation)
“...manifestation of countless bodies, as many as all the atomic particles in the plane of dharma.” — Gomez, p. 163 (course packet translation)

Interpretation: number inflation is a literary strategy that dislocates ordinary scale and prepares a cosmic ethical horizon.

旅程 Sudhana's Teacher Network
“One by one, each of fifty-two teachers refers Sudhana to another spiritual friend.” — Gomez, p. 164 (course packet translation)
“...the fifty-third teacher, whose very name, Samantabhadra, betokens the bodhisattva’s perfect conduct.” — Gomez, p. 164 (course packet translation)

Interpretation: serial mentorship replaces single-master authority. Knowledge is distributed across encounters.

包容 Inclusion and Social Boundaries
“...his teachers... include laymen, laywomen, Brahmins, and female nightspirits.” — Gomez, p. 164 (course packet translation)
“The women among Sudhana’s teachers do not have to become men; lay persons do not have to become ascetics...” — Gomez, pp. 164-165 (course packet translation)

Classroom bridge: use this frame when discussing Vasumitra (courtesan traditions in Gandavyuha materials).

課程 20-Minute Seminar Flow

Ready-to-Teach Structure

導入 0-4 min

Prompt: What effect does “countless” have that a fixed number does not?

精讀 4-11 min

Read the three quote cards aloud. Students mark one repeated rhetoric pattern (scale, list, referral, inclusion).

小組 11-17 min

Pairs choose one quote and produce a two-sentence claim linking literary form and ethics.

收束 17-20 min

Exit ticket: How do infinity language and many teachers reshape who can awaken and who can teach?

討論 Discussion Questions

Text-Tied Questions

問題 Seminar Questions
  1. Using the quote on p. 163, how does “as many as all the atomic particles” function rhetorically rather than mathematically?
  2. What changes when Sudhana moves through fifty-three teachers instead of receiving one final doctrinal lecture?
  3. How do pp. 164-165 challenge monastic and gender hierarchy through the roster of teachers?
  4. Where does the text frame spiritual authority as relational instead of institutional?
